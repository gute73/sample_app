Marquee Rotators Usage

A marquee rotator is a multimedia presentation where you can upload multiple slides, control slide transitions, include music, and create links from the slides to another webpage. A marquee rotator can be attached to the marquee on any page of your website. The Marquee Rotators area is used to view and manage marquee rotators.

Managing Marquee Rotators:

  • Log in to your SITE MANAGER > hover over CONTENT >  click on MARQUEE ROTATORS under MEDIA.
    • This displays all of the marquee rotators that have been created
  • Use the Marquee Rotators tab to add, edit, or delete your marquee rotators.




Adding a Marquee Rotator:
  1. Click Add or click on the title of an existing rotator.
  2. Fill in the appropriate fields. Only the Title, Width, and Height are required fields.
    • Enter a title for the marquee rotator. This title will only appear on the Site Manager and not on your live website.
    • Enter the width (in pixels) of the marquee rotator.
    • Enter the height (in pixels) of the marquee rotator.
  3. Save the page.
Adding, Editing, Enhancing, and Deleting Slides:
  • Click on the Slides tab to add, edit, enhance, or delete slides for your rotators.
  • Choose the rotator that you want to view the slides of in the Marquee Rotator drop down box.
  • Adding a Slide: Click on the green Add button.
  • Editing a Slide: Click the title of the slide or click Edit under the slide title.
  • Enhancing a Slide: Click Enhance under the slide title.
    • Enhancing includes resizing, adding text, etc.
  • Deleting a Slide: Click Delete under the slide title > confirm when prompted.  
  • Deleting Multiple Slides: Check the boxes of the slides you want to delete > click the red Delete button that appears at the top of the page.
Individual Slides:
  1. Title: Type a title for the slide. This title will only appear in your Site Manager.
  2. Start and End Date: Add a start date and end date for the slide. The start date is the date the slide will appear. The end date is the last day the slide will display. This field is optional. If no dates are entered, the slide will display until you delete it or deactivate it.
  3. Image: Only .jpg image files can be used.
    • Drag the image into the Drop Image Here box or click the Select Image button.
    • Select a file from your computer > click Open. 
    • Drag the selection window to the preferred area on your image.
    • Drag the corners of the selection window as much as it will allow you to enlarge the window. The correct dimensions will be retained.
    • Click the Crop button.
      • Click the Undo button > re-crop the image if you do not like the way the image was cropped.
      • Click Select Image to choose a new image if you want to change your image selection.
    • Click Save once you are satisfied with the cropped result.
  4. Transition: Choose how you want the slide to transition from one slide to another. Fade In Transition is the default one used.
  5. Display Duration (Seconds): Enter the number of seconds you want the slide to appear for before transitioning to the next slide.
  6. Link URL: Paste a link URL into this window if you want to attach a link to the slide.
    • Click the link icon next to the link URL field if you are linking to a page on your website.
    • Click PAGES > select the page you want the slide to link to in the pop up window.
  7. Link Target: Choose this if you want the link to open in a new window or the same window.
  8. Deactivated: Check this box if you want to deactivate the slide.
  9. Click Save at the bottom of the page.

Displaying a Marquee Rotator on Your Pages:

  1. Log in to your SITE MANAGER.
  2. Click on PAGES.
  3. Click Edit under the page you are adding/changing the Marquee Rotator on.
  4. Click on the Attachable Content tab.
  5. Click on the Attach Marquee Rotator link.
  6. Choose the name of the Marquee Rotator that you want to attach to the homepage in the drop box.
  7. Click Save at the bottom of the page.
  • Images must be at least as large as the rotator dimensions in order to be uploaded to a slide.
  • We recommend you do not upload large images straight from your digital camera. Images from a digital camera can be very large and need to be resized before uploading them to your website.
  • We recommend you do not use filenames with spaces or special characters in them. Spaces or special characters in filenames may cause those images not to display on some computers and browsers.
  • If you are changing the Marquee Rotator from the default one created by us, make sure the new one has the same pixel dimensions as the default.

Creating Marquee Rotator Slides

  • Images that are not the same dimensions or are at least as large as the dimensions of the marquee rotator cannot be uploaded to the rotator slide.
  1. Log in to your SITE MANAGER.
  2. Hover over CONTENT > click on MARQUEE ROTATORS in your Site Manager.
  3. Click on the title of the Marquee Rotator that you are creating slides for.
  4. Verify the Canvas Width and Canvas Height (in pixels).
    • Verify the marquee rotator dimensions before creating or changing a slide.
Adding or Editing a Slide
  1. Hover over CONTENT > click on MARQUEE ROTATORS in your Site Manager.
  2. Click on the Slides tab.
  3. Choose the rotator you are adding the image to in the Marquee Rotator dropdown box.
    • This displays all of the slides for the marquee rotator you chose.
  4. If you are switching out an image of an existing slide, click Edit on the slide you wish to change > skip down to step 7.
  5. Click Add.
  6. Fill in the Title, Image, and Display Duration fields.
    • These fields are required; all other fields are optional.
    • Title: Type a title for the slide. This title only appears in the slides viewing table.
    • Image:
      1. Click the Select Image button.
      2. Select your file in the file dialog window > click Open.
        • This will load your image into the image selection area.
      3. Drag the selection window to the preferred area on your image.
      4. Drag the corners of the selection window as much as it will allow you to enlarge the window.
        • The correct marquee dimensions will be retained.
      5. Click Crop.
      6. Click Save if you are satisfied with the cropped image.
        • Click Undo > repeat steps 3 and 4 to re-crop the image.
        • Click the Select Image button > choose a new image to change your image selection.
    • Display Duration (Seconds): Enter the number of seconds you want the slide to appear for before transitioning to the next slide.
  7. Click Save at the bottom of the page.
  • We not not recommend uploading images straight from your digital camera. They can be very large and need to be resized before uploading them to your website.
  • We do not recommend using filenames with spaces or special characters in them. These may cause those images not to display on some computers and browsers.
  • Click the Marquee Rotators button to go back to the main Marquee Rotator viewing table.

Attach a Marquee Rotator to a Page

  1. Log in to your Site Manager.
  2. Click on PAGES.
  3. Click the title of the page you want to attach the marquee rotator to.
  4. Click on the Attachable Content tab.
  5. Click on Attach Marquee Rotator.
  6. Select the title of the desired marquee rotator from the drop-down list.
  7. Click Save at the bottom of the page.

Important Note

  • Visit "Creating Marquee Rotator Slides" for instructions on creating marquee rotators.

Attaching a Link to a Marquee Rotator Slide

You can attach a link to a page, file, download, outside website, etc., if you have the URL.
  1. Log in to your SITE MANAGER.
  2. Click on WEBSITE > MARQUEE ROTATORS under MEDIA > SLIDES.
  3. Select the set of Marquee Rotators that you would like to edit under Marquee Rotator.
  4. Click on the Title of the slide that you are going to attach the link to.
    • This will display a Link URL box.
    • Linking to an Outside Site: Paste the outside website URL in the Link URL box.
  5. Click on the icon next to the link URL box if you are going to link to a file from the file library, a site page, or a download.
    • A pop up window will appear and give you access to your File Library, Site Pages, and Downloads.
  6. Upload and select a file or click on a page or download that you want the slide to link to.
  7. Link Target box: Click on the appropriate button to specify whether the link opens up in the same browser window or in a new browser window.
  8. Click Save at the bottom of your slide.

Changing the Sort Order of Marquee Rotator Slides

  1. Log in to your SITE MANAGER > scroll over the CONTENT tab > click on Marquee Rotators under Media.
  2. Click on the Slides tab.
  3. Select the rotator you want to change under Marquee Rotator.
  4. Click and hold on the Expand icon to the left of slide title (looks like 5 lines) > drag it to wherever you want to place the slide within the slide ordering system.
  • Changes will save automatically.

Subpage Marquee Graphic

Many of our templates feature a primary marquee graphic, which is typically affixed to the top of the page. There are two ways to populate the subpage marquee area.
  1. Create and attach a Marquee Rotator for transitioning slides.
  2. Attach a single image to a page.
Attaching a Marquee Rotator
  1. Log in to your SITE MANAGER.
  2. Hover over CONTENT > click on MARQUEE ROTATORS under MEDIA.
  3. Click Add to create a new Marquee Rotator.
  4. Click the Slides tab > select the new rotator in the drop-down > click Add to add new slides.
  5. Click on PAGES > click the page title of the page the rotator will be attached to.
  6. Click on the Attachable Content tab > scroll down to the Attach Marquee Rotator section > click the grab handle  to expand it. 
  7. Select the Marquee Rotator you want to attach in the dropdown box > save the page.
Attaching a Single Image
  1. Click on PAGES.
  2. Click the page title of the page you want to add a marquee image to.
  3. Find the Marquee Image area located at the bottom of the page.
  4. Click the Select Image button.
  5. Select your file in the file dialog window > click Open. This will load your image into the marquee image selection area.
  6. Drag the selection window to the preferred area on your image. 
    • You can drag the corners of the selection window as necessary to enlarge the window, and the correct marquee dimensions will be retained.
  7. Click the crop button.
  8. Click the Save button if you like how the picture looks cropped.
    • Click the Undo button > repeat steps 6 and 7 if you want to re-crop the image. 
    • Click the Select Image button to choose a new image if you want to change your image selection.
  9. Save the page.
  • The single image will now appear on the page it is specified for, and it will always appear on that page unless you remove it from the page.
  • Images must be the at least the same pixel dimensions or larger than the size of the page marquee area.
  • The dimensions of your page marquee area are listed in the Marquee Image description at the bottom of any given page. Use these dimensions to appropriately size subpage Marquee Rotators.
  • The files must be saved in JPG format. Associated file names cannot contain spaces or special characters (except "_" or "-").
